ECO4 Scheme

Free energy efficiency improvements for low-income and vulnerable households

About ECO4

The Energy Company Obligation (ECO4) scheme helps low-income households improve their home's energy efficiency through free insulation, heating upgrades, and other energy-saving measures.

Who Qualifies?

  • Households receiving certain benefits
  • Low-income households meeting specific criteria
  • Vulnerable residents
  • Private tenants (with landlord permission)

Available Measures

  • Loft insulation
  • Cavity wall insulation
  • Solid wall insulation
  • Heating system upgrades
  • Renewable heating systems
